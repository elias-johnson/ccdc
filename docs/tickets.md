# Tickets
Creating tickets is a essential part of playing, as under utilizing the services that the black team offers you can result in the loss of many more points then the cost of the ticket.

## Ticket Types
- Service Scoring Validation
  0 points (free!), but they’ll cut you off if you abuse it.
  If you believe your service is working 100% correctly but is not being scored correctly,
  and you want a human to double check the scoring engine, file this ticket. If it’s used
  frequently without additional consultations, they will require a Service Scoring Check
  ticket at minimum.
- Box Reset / Scrub
  60 points or 100 points for your router
  They will reset your box to start of competition state and notify you when it is ready
- Scoring Service Check
  10 points
  Have black team provide some additional context surrounding the service check (details on the failure).
- Black Team Phone Consultation
  100 points
  Have black team diagnose your issue over a voice call with you (through Discord)
- Black Team Hands on Consultation
  200 points
  Have black team gain access to your box to investigate and describe the issue to you, attempting to to fix things along the way

## Other Advice
Remember, SLA checks are 25 points every 5 minutes. A box reset is worth less then a mere 15 minutes of being down, and a black team phone consultation is worth about 20 minutes.
