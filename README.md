# CCDC Playbook
The intention of this repo is to house a generalized playbook to aid
George Fox University students in the Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC).

## What is [PR]CCDC?
PRCCDC (_Pacific Rim_ Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition) is a competition where multiple blue teams (the competitors) are given several machines that run graded services. Throughout the competition, the blue teams will have opportunities to both earn and lose points. The points are earned through numerous different methods, and the ways you earn points may vary, so it is important to understand exactly how you get and lose points.

### Point Breakdown
The actual amount of points you get for doing certain tasks may vary, but what those tasks are generally remain the same year after year.
* Service Uptime - Every minute or two, an uptime check will run on each of your services (which you will have roughly 25 of). If you fail *5* uptime checks in a row, you will receive an SLA violation, reducing your points by 25. Having your uptime checks pass will net you some amount of points.

* Injects - Every hour or so, your team will receive an inject. These are essentially "business tasks," which generally speaking can be almost anything. These are also worth varying amounts of points.

* Red Team Deductions - Throughout the competition, the red team will be continuously attempting to break into your systems. If they can, and what they find when they do, will result in various point deductions.

* Orange Team - While Orange Team is responsible for the injects, they are also going to be the ones you will be speaking to over the phone and through Discord. You will be graded through various metrics on those, but this is your opportunity to have fun!

## Why This Guide?
While the competetion various year to year, there are certian parts of it that remian the same. This guide hopes to best prepare you and your team so you know what to expect. Here you can find useful [Scripts](scripts) and [Docs](docs) that will help with understanding certain services.
